How to write Natural-Language Queries

Query Type Number of Stats Qualifiers Periods Key Features & Examples
Basic Single Stat ✓ ✓ Ideal for simple searches.
"Players with Russian yards greater than 10."
Complex 1 to 3 Stats ✓ ✓ Combine multiple stats with 'and' or 'or'.
Example: "Teams with at least 20 passing attempts AND three field goals made in a game."
Records Single Stat ✓ ✓ Find highest/lowest values.
Example: "Highest number of points scored." (Note: Multiple stats don't make sense here.)
Last Time Up to 3 Stats ✓ ✓ Pinpoint the last occurrence of specific conditions.
Example: "Last time any team had at least one field goal made with overall 30 points."
Streaks 1 or 2 Stats ✗ ✓ Analyze consecutive performances.
Example: "Player with a streak of 3 games with at least 1 touchdown."
Note: Limited to game periods for logical consistency.
